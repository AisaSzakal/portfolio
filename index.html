Working with Colorbar
Colorbar is a store that collects vintage and designer furniture, for people, who are planning to decorate their homes uniquely.
My job was to put together selling items, and take pictures, and small clips of them,
then before publishing it I had to edit everything to bring out the same atmosphere of every post.
